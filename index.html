The Project

Ban Ko Prae school, located in the jungle of Mae Hong Son province, must be relocated due to severe landslide risks at its current site. The school is moving to a new location 6 km away. This project funds the construction of a water reservoir close to the new school site and the water supply connection that serves it — ensuring the relocated school has safe, reliable access to water from day one.

02

Location & Access

From the current school, the route retraces 4 km back along the access road, then climbs 2 km up the hill to the new site. The water reservoir is built close to the new school location, feeding the supply directly.
